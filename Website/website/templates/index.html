Who We Are

We are Pawns Pathways, a nonprofit organization dedicated to the promotion, education, and accessibility of the game of chess.

Our team consists of passionate chess enthusiasts, professional players, and educators from high school who believe in the power of chess to transform lives.

We collaborate with communities, schools, and other organizations to spread the love for this ancient game.

What We Do

Train
Learn chess from the beginning. We are here to guide you on your chess journey, starting from the very basics. Our top-quality education is absolutely free for all children.

Compete
Our training program ensures you're well-prepared to compete in chess tournaments, engage in volunteering, and more. These skills are not just about chess; they are essential for life.

Reflect
We encourage all our students to reflect on their chess milestones and life skills development. Chess isn't just a game; it's a path to personal growth.
