Welcome to Pawns Pathways

Here at Pawns Pathways we provide intensive chess training of all levels to children for free, guiding them each and every step of their journey.

Chess teaches children valuable life
skills including critical thinking, patience, and perserverance.

We make it our mission to strengthen these skills for all children, fostering tomorrow's skills today.

Who are we?

We are Pawns Pathways, a nonprofit organization dedicated to the promotion, education, and accessibility of the game of chess. Our team consists of passionate chess enthusiasts, professional players, and educators from high school who believe in the power of chess to transform lives. We collaborate with communities, schools, and other organizations to spread the love for this ancient game.

What do we do?

  1. Train
    We will train you from your first step in chess till you become an advanced level player. We ensure the highest quality of education for free to all children.

  2. Compete
    As part of our training we make sure you become ready to compete in tournaments, volunteering, etc. We believe these valuable skills is an essential part of chess.

  3. Reflect
    We make sure all students reflect on all milestones of their chess journey to ensure they don't only get better at chess, but many other essential life skills.
